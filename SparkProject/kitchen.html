    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

    Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a large bowl add sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt
  3. Mix.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla.
  5. Mix.
  6. Add boiling water.
  7. Mix.
  8. Pour into cake pans.
  9. Bake for 30 minutes.
