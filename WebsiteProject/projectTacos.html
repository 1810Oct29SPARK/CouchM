Viva los tacos.

Are you ready to put your local Taco Bell to shame? Does the idea of blaring 'Despacito' outside the Trump tower make you feel some type of way? Look no further, amigo.

Supplies

  • large frying pan
  • fine mesh colander

Step-by-step how-to

  1. Cook the ground beef fully.
  2. Drain Grease.
  3. Add 1/2 cup tomato sauce and all seasoning.
  4. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve it up.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef (70-80% lean)
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
