Supplies

  • cake pan
  • mixing bowl
  • whisk
  • wooden spoon
  • ziplock bag

Step-by-step how-to

The Cookie:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Grease a 9 inch cake pan.
  3. Cream the butter and sugars together for 2 minutes and then mix in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla.
  4. Add the dry ingredients and mix until combined.
  5. Use a wooden spoon to mix in all but 2 tablespoons of chocolate chips and then press the cookie dough into the pie plate.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining 2 tablespoons chocolate chips on top and press them down slightly.
  7. Bake for 22-25 minutes.
  8. Let the cookie cool completely and then run a sharp knife along the edge to loosen and then use a large spatula (or two!) to transfer to a platter.
  9. Pipe on frosting along the edges and add the sprinkles.

The Frosting:

  1. Beat all the frosting ingredients together until smooth.
  2. Add milk as needed.
  3. Pour into a ziplock bag, tearing a hole into one of the bottom corners.
  4. Pipe on frosting.
  5. Throw the sprinkles all up on there.

Ingredients:

For the Cookie:

  • 10 tbsp butter
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 & 2/3 cup flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • Love.

For the Frosting:

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 & 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp milk
  • Sprinkles
