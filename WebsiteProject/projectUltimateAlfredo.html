Making a great thing even better...

Extrapolating on simple Alfredo, this recipe takes everything good about alfredo sauce, and makes it all the more amazing.

Supplies

  • medium sauce pan
  • whisk

Step-by-step how-to

  1. Add the cream and butter to a sauce pan over medium heat and bring to a simmer.
  2. Whisk in the garlic and simmer for 30 seconds.
  3. Turn off the heat and whisk in the Parmesan cheese, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  4. Continue whisking until cheese has fully melted and sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Serve immediately.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • a dash of nutmeg
